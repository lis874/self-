“I bought this book back in 2018, when I traveled to Tokyo with my friend during summer break. It was right before I was going back to NYC after taking a gap year, so I was excited to explore Japanese culture, which is pretty different from Korea. It is also another kind of exotic experience than the ones I had in the western countries. My friend and I randomly visited a bookstore and there I found this book.

The night sky and moon are something that I emotionally connect with. It’s my favorite time during the day when I could focus on myself with calm and peaceful music.{ 1 } The book recalls both ‘night time’ and ‘summer break with my friend’ that I enjoyed a lot. So whenever I get exhausted from my routine, I always gaze at this book without thinking anything.” Maddie Lee
