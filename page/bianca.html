“They exist as a promise I made to my God. There’s three of them;

1. One that I received as my original ring, which says; “I will wait for my beloved”.

2. One that exists as a placeholder for a wedding ring.

3. One that I wear with the placeholder ring that has the word “eternity” inscribed in it. My sister and I made it this past year as a reminder during these difficult times of the coming fulfillment of God’s promise of the future.

These rings are more than a purity ring to me, they stand as an oath I swore when I turned away from my old life’s choices.{ 1 } I do not get attached to physical things almost ever but these are special to me.” Bianca Criscuolo
